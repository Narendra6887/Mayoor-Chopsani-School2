Campus Life


Labs

We have well equipped laboratories, with customized equipments to cater to the requirements of different curricula and scholars of different age groups. In addition to the Physics, Chemistry and Biology Labs, there are separate Robotics and Computer Labs as well.
Buses

The Transport facility currently includes 16 buses and 25 vans that are equipped with a Vehicle Tracking System (VTS) and on board cameras. This allows the School to monitor speed and location of the route. This effectively ensures the safety and security of scholars on board.


Playgrounds

The school participates in district, state and national level competitions in a variety of sports.
